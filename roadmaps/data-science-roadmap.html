Career Roadmap

Data Science Pathway

A step-by-step path to become a data-driven problem solver ready for internships and real-world roles.

Skill Depth: Beginner to Advanced Weekly Effort: 8 to 12 Hours Goal: Build Proof of Work
01

Math and Python Foundations

Build a strong base in Python and core mathematics used in machine learning.

  • Python basics, functions, modules, and data structures.
  • Linear algebra, probability, statistics, and calculus essentials.
  • Practice numerical problems and Python mini exercises.
  • Primary outcome: complete one measurable deliverable for this phase.
  • Practice target: allocate 5 sessions per week with revision on weekends.
  • Proof of work: publish notes, artifacts, or demos in a portfolio folder.

Checkpoint: move ahead only after one self-test or mentor review.

Timeline: Weeks 1 to 6
02

Data Analysis and Visualization

Learn how to clean, explore, and present insights from raw data effectively.

  • NumPy, Pandas, and data wrangling workflows.
  • Matplotlib, Seaborn, and dashboard storytelling.
  • Exploratory data analysis on public datasets.
  • Primary outcome: complete one measurable deliverable for this phase.
  • Practice target: allocate 5 sessions per week with revision on weekends.
  • Proof of work: publish notes, artifacts, or demos in a portfolio folder.

Checkpoint: move ahead only after one self-test or mentor review.

Timeline: Weeks 7 to 12
03

Machine Learning Core

Understand model training, evaluation, and bias-variance trade-offs.

  • Supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms.
  • Scikit-learn pipelines, feature engineering, and tuning.
  • Metrics: precision, recall, F1, ROC-AUC, RMSE.
  • Primary outcome: complete one measurable deliverable for this phase.
  • Practice target: allocate 5 sessions per week with revision on weekends.
  • Proof of work: publish notes, artifacts, or demos in a portfolio folder.

Checkpoint: move ahead only after one self-test or mentor review.

Timeline: Weeks 13 to 20
04

Databases and Data Engineering Basics

Work with production-style datasets and reliable data pipelines.

  • SQL queries, joins, window functions, and optimization.
  • ETL concepts and data pipeline fundamentals.
  • Versioning datasets and reproducible notebooks.
  • Primary outcome: complete one measurable deliverable for this phase.
  • Practice target: allocate 5 sessions per week with revision on weekends.
  • Proof of work: publish notes, artifacts, or demos in a portfolio folder.

Checkpoint: move ahead only after one self-test or mentor review.

Timeline: Weeks 21 to 26
05

Advanced Topics and Deployment

Take models beyond notebooks and deploy practical solutions.

  • Intro to deep learning with TensorFlow or PyTorch.
  • Model serving with Flask or FastAPI.
  • Basic MLOps, monitoring, and model lifecycle.
  • Primary outcome: complete one measurable deliverable for this phase.
  • Practice target: allocate 5 sessions per week with revision on weekends.
  • Proof of work: publish notes, artifacts, or demos in a portfolio folder.

Checkpoint: move ahead only after one self-test or mentor review.

Timeline: Weeks 27 to 34
06

Portfolio and Interview Readiness

Prepare for roles with polished projects and business-focused communication.

  • Build 3 strong projects (NLP, prediction, analytics).
  • Document case studies with data and impact.
  • Practice SQL, ML theory, and product-thinking questions.
  • Primary outcome: complete one measurable deliverable for this phase.
  • Practice target: allocate 5 sessions per week with revision on weekends.
  • Proof of work: publish notes, artifacts, or demos in a portfolio folder.

Checkpoint: move ahead only after one self-test or mentor review.

Timeline: Weeks 35 onwards
